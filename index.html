NBA Analytics

The following analysis has been done on every NBA player for every season up until 2022. We inspected a variety of attributes that can contribute to player performance as well as team performance. The original intent of this project was to find true odds of a player or team's performance. Although still possible, the analysis is now geared towards identifying trends within the sport for predictive purposes. Please follow the navigation on the side for more!
